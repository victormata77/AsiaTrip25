Our Destinations

A carefully crafted journey through Asia's most captivating places

Seoul, South Korea

July 31 - August 4

Where ancient palaces stand beside futuristic skyscrapers. Experience vibrant night markets, K-culture, and the DMZ.

Tokyo, Japan

August 4 - 8

Immerse yourself in Tokyo's electric energy, from peaceful shrines to the world's busiest crossing and digital art museums.

Kyoto, Japan

August 8 - 10

Step back in time with ancient temples, traditional tea houses, bamboo forests, and the possibility of geisha sightings.

Singapore

August 11 - 14

Experience the perfect blend of cultures in this garden city, with futuristic attractions and luxury resort amenities.

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

August 14 - 16

Marvel at the iconic Petronas Towers, explore cultural sites like Batu Caves, and savor Malaysian street food.

Maldives

August 16 - 20

Unwind in paradise with overwater villas, crystal-clear waters for snorkeling, and pristine white beaches.
