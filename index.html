Our Itinerary

Grouped by countries for an easy overview of our adventure

Barcelona to Seoul (July 30 - August 4)

Departure from Barcelona

Overnight flight from Barcelona to Seoul, South Korea

Seoul Highlights (4 nights)

  • Cultural immersion at Gyeongbokgung Palace with traditional guard changing ceremony
  • Exploration of Bukchon Hanok Village with traditional Korean houses
  • Full-day tour to the DMZ (Demilitarized Zone) and JSA
  • Split activities: K-Beauty experiences for the girls and tech experiences for the boys
  • Visit to Lotte World theme park or N Seoul Tower
  • Authentic Korean cuisine at Gwangjang Market

Japan: Tokyo & Kyoto (August 4 - 10)

Tokyo Highlights (4 nights)

  • Meiji Shrine, Yoyogi Park, and the fashion district of Harajuku
  • Experience the famous Shibuya Crossing and Shibuya Sky observation deck
  • Choice of Samurai Museum or Anime Workshop
  • Ancient Sensō-ji Temple and modern Tokyo Skytree
  • Digital art immersion at teamLab Planets or Borderless
  • Food tour at Tsukiji Outer Market followed by fresh sushi lunch
  • Traditional Japanese tea ceremony experience

Kyoto Highlights (2 nights)

  • Bullet train experience from Tokyo to Kyoto
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine with thousands of vermilion torii gates
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) reflected in a tranquil pond
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and Tenryu-ji Temple gardens
  • Kimono/Yukata wearing experience
  • Evening walk through historic Gion District with potential geisha sightings
  • Visit to Kyoto Imperial Palace and Nijo Castle with nightingale floors

Singapore & Malaysia (August 11 - 16)

Singapore Highlights (3 nights)

  • Stay at Village Hotel Sentosa with four themed pools
  • Sentosa Island attractions (Universal Studios or S.E.A. Aquarium)
  • Gardens by the Bay with Supertree Grove and conservatories
  • Cultural Heritage Tour through Chinatown and Little India
  • Marina Bay Sands SkyPark for panoramic city views
  • ArtScience Museum's Future World exhibition

Kuala Lumpur Highlights (2 nights)

  • Luxury stay at The Ritz-Carlton, Kuala Lumpur
  • Petronas Twin Towers visit and observation deck
  • Batu Caves with 272 colorful steps and Hindu temple complex
  • Shopping at Central Market or canopy walk at KL Forest Eco Park
  • Authentic street food experience at Jalan Alor Night Food Street

Maldives & Return Journey (August 16 - 21)

Maldives Paradise (4 nights)

  • Scenic seaplane transfer to Radisson Blu Resort Maldives
  • Luxury overwater villa accommodation
  • Snorkeling on the house reef with abundant marine life
  • Water sports activities (kayaking, paddleboarding, jet skiing)
  • Spa treatments with panoramic ocean views
  • Private beach areas and infinity pool relaxation

Return to Barcelona (August 20-21)

  • Flight from Malé to Singapore
  • Overnight flight from Singapore to Barcelona
  • Early morning arrival in Barcelona
