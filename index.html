Our Itinerary

Simplified overview of our adventure

Barcelona to Seoul July 30 - August 4

  • Overnight flight from Barcelona to Seoul
  • Cultural immersion at Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • Explore traditional Bukchon Hanok Village
  • DMZ tour to witness Korean history
  • K-Beauty experiences & tech adventures
  • Authentic street food at local markets

Accommodation: UH Suite The Coex (4 nights)

Japan: Tokyo & Kyoto August 4 - 11

Tokyo (4 nights):

  • Meiji Shrine & trendy Harajuku district
  • Iconic Shibuya Crossing experience
  • Samurai Museum or Anime Workshop
  • Digital art immersion at teamLab
  • Traditional tea ceremony

Kyoto (2 nights):

  • Bullet train from Tokyo to Kyoto
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine with vermilion gates
  • Golden Pavilion & Bamboo Grove
  • Kimono wearing experience
  • Evening walk through historic Gion District

Narita Airport (1 night):

  • Return to Tokyo via bullet train
  • Transit hotel at Narita Airport
  • Convenient for early morning flight

Accommodation: MIMARU SUITES Tokyo & Kyoto, 9h nine hours Narita Airport

Singapore & Malaysia August 11 - 16

Singapore (3 nights):

  • Sentosa Island resort with themed pools
  • Gardens by the Bay with Supertree Grove
  • Cultural exploration of diverse neighborhoods
  • Marina Bay Sands SkyPark views
  • Universal Studios or S.E.A. Aquarium

Kuala Lumpur (2 nights):

  • Petronas Twin Towers visit
  • Colorful Batu Caves Hindu temple
  • Local markets and shopping
  • Authentic street food experience

Accommodation: Village Hotel Sentosa & The Ritz-Carlton

Maldives & Return Journey August 16 - 21

Maldives (4 nights):

  • Scenic seaplane transfer to private resort
  • Luxury overwater villa accommodation
  • Snorkeling in crystal-clear waters
  • Water sports activities & spa treatments
  • Beach relaxation & family time

Return to Barcelona:

  • Flights from Malé to Singapore to Barcelona
  • Morning arrival in Barcelona on August 21

Accommodation: Radisson Blu Resort Maldives
